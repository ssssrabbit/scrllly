Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how scrllly ("the App") handles your data. We believe in keeping things simple and secure.

1. Data Collection

scrllly is a standalone desktop application. We do not collect, store, track, or transmit any personal information, usage analytics, or presentation scripts to our servers or any third parties. All processing happens locally on your Mac.

Any text, scripts, or files you load into scrllly remain entirely on your device. We do not have access to your content.
