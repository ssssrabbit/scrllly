About scrllly

scrllly is a powerful, straightforward teleprompter application designed exclusively for macOS. Whether you are recording a video presentation, giving a live speech, or streaming, scrllly ensures your delivery is flawless and perfectly timed.

Simply paste your script, set the exact duration of your presentation, and scrllly will automatically scroll your text from start to finish. No more guessing your pacing.

Key Features

  • Precision Timing: Specify the exact duration in seconds, and the app will scroll the entire script seamlessly within that timeframe.
  • Mirror Mode: Flip the text horizontally to use with physical teleprompter glass rigs.
  • Customizable Typography: Easily change font types and sizes to ensure perfect readability at any distance.
  • Rich Text Highlighting: Supports basic HTML tags like <b> for bold and <i> for italics, allowing you to emphasize key delivery points.
  • Quick Skip: Missed a beat? Use the Left and Right arrow keys to instantly skip backward or forward by 5 seconds.
  • External Display Support: Output your script directly to an external monitor or teleprompter screen.
  • Focus Reading Line: Keep your eyes locked on the right words with an adjustable red indicator line (customizable line width).
  • Light & Dark Modes: Choose the theme that fits your environment and reduces eye strain.
  • Multilingual Support: Interface available in English, Japanese, Korean, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Spanish, and Portuguese.

Pricing

Free Tier Available

You can use scrllly entirely for free on your Mac's main display for scripts that are 60 seconds or shorter.

For presentations exceeding 60 seconds, or to unlock external display capabilities without limitations, a premium upgrade is required.
